TEAM Automation Berlin is present around the world thanks to its three locations in Berlin (DE), Poznań (PL) and Tampa (USA), as well as its international service partners in the USA, Mexico and China. The teams in Berlin and Poznań develop and implement customized automation solutions for various industries. Thanks to strategic partnerships, global rapid and professional service is also ensured – directly on site and at the highest quality. Whether in Europe, America or Asia – TEAM Automation Berlin supports you with its expertise, innovative technologies and solutions tailored to your needs.

TEAM Automation Berlin GmbH

As an engineering company, TEAM Automation Berlin GmbH has been specializing for about 20 years in designing and constructing bespoke machines and devices worldwide. Customized solutions support clients in the areas of measurement, testing, control and regulation, especially in automating production processes. Based on individual customer requirements, flexible and efficient automation solutions are developed that integrate the necessary processes into existing workflows and often deliver economic benefits. In executing projects, the company relies on innovative technologies and works closely with component manufacturers. Our employees are also specialized in optimizing and upgrading both in-house and external equipment.

From an engineering office to a medium-sized company

Starting as an engineering office, over the years we have gained valuable competencies and experience that have enabled stable growth. Along with changes in partners and management, the company name was changed in 2006. Today, TEAM Automation Berlin is an efficient and competitive medium-sized company, present on international markets along with its subsidiaries. To provide global customer service, partnerships with local, audited companies have been established.

Main headquarters in Berlin-Tempelhof

Located directly next to the A100 (Berlin beltway), the company headquarters offers over 3000 m² of office and assembly space, where employees from various departments develop innovative solutions. In addition to many years of experience in the automotive sector, TEAM Automation Berlin also offers its expertise in coil winding technology and image recognition.
