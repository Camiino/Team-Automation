Semi-Automatic Systems

We create semi-automatic assembly systems according to customer requirements and using various processes, in which the placement and feeding of production parts is performed manually. These systems can later be fully automated if needed by retrofitting appropriate automatic material feeding units.

System Parameters

  • Cycle time per individual battery: 2.5 seconds
  • Feeding, testing, and aligning of individual batteries
  • Configuring different battery packs containing 10, 20, or 40 individual batteries

System Parameters

  • Cycle time per shaft: 40 seconds
  • Manual loading and unloading of parts
  • Automatic greasing, bolting, and labeling of parts
