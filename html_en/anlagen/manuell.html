Manual Workstations

According to your requirements, we create manual workstations ranging from the simplest preassembly and assembly tasks to complex test or packaging stations, possibly also in conjunction with highly flexible production systems.

System Parameters

  • Cycle time per display: 60 seconds
  • Place housing into fixture and secure it
  • The housing is automatically rotated radially to the respective processing station
  • The assembly process/sequence is displayed digitally
  • Testing processes start automatically
  • After testing, OK/NOK parts are displayed

System Parameters

  • Cycle time per part: 25 seconds
  • Manually insert part into fixture nest
  • Manually secure and contact the part
  • Start the testing process
  • After testing, OK/NOK parts are displayed

System Parameters

  • Semi- and fully automated assembly systems for exhaust throttle valves
  • Semi- and fully automated assembly systems for electronic actuators
  • End-of-line tester
  • PCB assembly systems
  • Plasma cleaning and coating system

TEAM Automation Berlin has been supplying well-known automotive suppliers worldwide since 2006 with semi- and fully automated assembly systems for exhaust throttle valves and actuators.
