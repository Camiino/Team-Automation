Service Portfolio

Concept, Planning, and Consulting

For all significant proposals, comprehensive technical and logistical feasibility assessments as well as detailed process and system concepts are prepared. To achieve optimal project outcomes for our clients, the engineers and specialists at TEAM Automation Berlin develop essential feasibility analyses, cycle time, and availability evaluations. TEAM Automation Berlin supports and advises its clients already during the product design phase, ensuring that the automation potential of production and assembly processes is fully assessed.

Feasibility Analyses

For all inquiries directed to TEAM Automation Berlin, technical and logistical feasibility analyses are prepared before drafting cost estimates and proposals — ensuring profitability and adherence to deadlines.

Cycle Time and Availability Analysis

Extensive analyses of cycle times and availability are conducted at every critical stage of development to achieve the targeted objectives and ensure client results.

Process and Production Concepts

The process and production concepts provided along with the proposals serve both as a visual tool for the client and as the basis for cost estimation.

Workstation Analysis

At the client’s request, TEAM Automation Berlin prepares detailed MTM analyses for both internal and external equipment.

Process Optimization

As a specialist in process automation, TEAM Automation Berlin supports its clients in all aspects related to optimizing processes and automated production cycles.

Project Implementation

Based on the developed concepts, TEAM Automation Berlin’s team implements concrete projects in close cooperation with the client – ranging from individual devices to semi- and fully automated systems. In the production of components and the delivery of parts, the company collaborates with long-standing partners from various industries who share high standards for quality and timeliness. All projects are monitored in terms of time and cost as part of comprehensive project management.

Mechanical Design

Specialists at TEAM Automation Berlin design and construct customized solutions, routinely preparing the following documentation:

• 3D Design
• 2D Assembly and Production Drawings
• GAB Plans
• System Layouts
• Parts Lists
• Spare and Operational Parts Lists

Electrical and Pneumatic Planning

In accordance with the client’s requirements and the results of the mechanical design, the electrotechnical/electronic department prepares CAE-supported documentation, including:

• Electrical and Pneumatic Schematics
• Control Cabinet Layouts and Component Placement Plans
• Cable Plans and Parts Lists
• Spare and Operational Parts Lists

Assembly of Control Cabinets and Wiring/Pneumatic Connections

TEAM Automation Berlin executes the construction of individual control cabinets up to complete automatic lines according to the prepared electrical and pneumatic documentation and tests them in accordance with applicable standards:

• DIN VDE 0100
• DIN EN 60439-1
• DIN EN 60204-1
• DIN EN 50178
• BGV A3
• European Standards
• UL Standards

For external devices, TEAM Automation Berlin assesses service and maintenance needs and quickly prepares appropriate offers.
