Systems

TEAM Automation Berlin systems offer customized solutions for various production requirements. From manual workstations through semi-automatic systems to fully automated lines that ensure maximum efficiency and precision. Regardless of the level of automation, all systems are characterized by quality, reliability, and optimal integration with your production processes.

Types of Systems

Manual Workstations

According to your requirements, TEAM Automation Berlin creates manual workstations for initial assembly and final assembly, as well as for more complex test or packaging stations that can also be used in conjunction with highly flexible production systems.

View examples

Semi-Automatic Systems

TEAM Automation Berlin creates semi-automatic assembly systems tailored to customer requirements, where the loading and delivery of components is done manually, while the processes themselves run automatically. These systems can later be fully automated.

View examples

Fully Automatic Systems

TEAM Automation Berlin designs and delivers fully automatic, highly advanced assembly systems, in which individual processes are interconnected using flexible transport systems or robots.
